More About Me

I’ve always been more interested in questions than answers. Science and rational thinking, for me, are not about being certain. They are about being willing to change your mind when the evidence politely disagrees.

Most of what I create comes from curiosity about everyday ideas we rarely stop to question. Why do some beliefs feel convincing even when they are wrong? Why does confidence sound so persuasive? And why is saying “I don’t know” treated like a personal failure?

This website and my content are small attempts to slow things down, think a little more carefully, and trust evidence more than enthusiasm. Mostly, I am just trying to understand things out loud.
